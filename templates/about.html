About GS Mobile

Innovating Mobile Technology Since 2015

How GS Started

GS Mobile began its journey in 2015 with a simple vision: to create affordable, high-quality smartphones that combine cutting-edge technology with elegant design. What started as a small startup in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, has now grown into one of India's most trusted mobile phone brands.

Our founders recognized a gap in the market for premium smartphones at accessible prices. With a team of passionate engineers and designers, GS Mobile set out to revolutionize the mobile industry by focusing on user experience, build quality, and innovative features.

Over the years, we have consistently delivered products that exceed customer expectations. From our first smartphone, the GS Alpha, to our latest flagship, the GS Mx 7, we have maintained our commitment to excellence and innovation.

Today, GS Mobile is proud to serve millions of customers across India and beyond, with a growing presence in international markets. Our success is built on trust, quality, and our unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction.

Meet Our Founder

Mr. Ganesh Sharma

Founder & CEO

Mr. Ganesh Sharma, a visionary entrepreneur and technology enthusiast, founded GS Mobile in 2015 with a dream to democratize smartphone technology in India. With over 15 years of experience in the telecommunications industry, he recognized the need for high-quality, affordable smartphones that could compete with international brands.

Born and raised in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, Mr. Sharma completed his engineering degree from IIT Madras and worked with several multinational companies before starting his entrepreneurial journey. His passion for innovation and commitment to excellence have been the driving forces behind GS Mobile's success.

Under his leadership, GS Mobile has grown from a small startup to a major player in the Indian smartphone market. His vision of "Technology for Everyone" continues to guide the company's mission to create accessible, cutting-edge mobile solutions.

"Every smartphone we create is a step towards bridging the digital divide and empowering people through technology," says Mr. Sharma about his company's mission.
